Procedure to migrate from jahia-hotfix-6.0.0_2_r27452 to Jahia_EE_V6_SP1_r28889 :

1. Remove the following jars from webapps\\ROOT\\WEB-INF\\lib\\
  • jahia-gwt-*.jar
  • jahia-impl-*.jar
  • jahia-taglib-*.jar
  • jahia-api-*.jar
  • jackrabbit-core-ext-*.jar
  • jackrabbit-server-ext-*.jar
and from webapps\\ROOT\\WEB-INF\\var\\shared_templates
  • web-templates-6.0-*.jar
  • test-templates-6.0*.jar
  • core-templates-6.0*.jar
2. Modify the following configuration file:
  • UnixDiff_web_xml.txt AND UnixDiff_web2_xml.txt for WEB-INF\\web.xml
  • UnixDiff_applicationcontext-hibernate_xml.txt for WEB-INF\\etc\\spring\\applicationcontext-hibernate.xml
  • UnixDiff_Applicationcontext-services_xml.txt AND UnixDiff_Applicationcontext-services2_xml.txt AND UnixDiff_Applicationcontext-services3_xml.txt for WEB-INF\\etc\\spring\\applicationcontext-services.xml
  • UnixDiff_applicationcontext-administration_xml.txt for WEB-INF\\etc\\spring\\applicationcontext-administration.xml
  • UnixDiff_applicationcontext-notification_xml.txt for WEB-INF\\etc\\spring\\applicationcontext-notification.xml
  • UnixDiff_ehcache-jahia_cluster_xml.txt for WEB-INF\\etc\\classes\\ehcache-jahia_cluster.xml
    3. Copy the content of the output folder into your webapps\\ROOT\\ folder
    4. Launch Jahia
